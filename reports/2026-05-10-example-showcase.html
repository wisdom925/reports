Why HTML, not markdown

Markdown reports are walls of headings and bullet points. They render the same on every platform — but that means they can't do the things below. This single file does all of them, with no build step.

Collapsibles for optional depth

Why <details> beats appendices

Readers who don't want the detail don't see it. Readers who do can expand inline without losing their place.

When NOT to use them

If the content is critical to the argument, don't hide it. Collapsibles are for elaboration, not load-bearing prose.
